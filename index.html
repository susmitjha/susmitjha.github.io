RESEARCH OVERVIEW AND FUNDING SUMMARY

Before joining DARPA, Dr. Susmit Jha was a Technical Director in the Computer Science Laboratory at SRI International, where he lead the research group on Neuro-symbolic Computing and Intelligence with research funded by DARPA, IARPA, ARPA-H, NSA, ARL, DLA, and NSF. His research focuses on trustworthy AI and formal methods for high-assurance applications such as autonomy, cyber-security, and AI-assisted design and engineering.

  • Over 100 peer-reviewed publications.
  • In the last 5 years, over 15 papers in top CSRankings venues such as NeurIPS, ICLR, ICML, AAAI, DAC, ICSE, CVPR, ICCV, TAI, TMLR, and JMLR.
  • Over 4500 citations; h-index 31; i10-index 65.
  • Secured over $26M as Principal Investigator and contributed to an additional $29M in funding.
