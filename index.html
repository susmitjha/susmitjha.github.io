    • Brief Bio
      • Susmit Jha is a Staff Research Scientist in Embedded Intelligence, Cyber-Physical Systems Group at United Technology Research Center, Berkeley. Susmit has been with UTRC since Dec, 2014. Susmit was a Research Scientist at Intel from Jan,2012-Dec,2014. Susmit has served on Program Committees of international venues on formal methods including Computer-Aided Verification, Formal Methods in Computer-Aided Design, Synthesis and Numerical Software Verification. Susmit is interested in combining formal methods and machine learning to build trusted artificial intelligence and correct-by-construction adaptive systems. Susmit is currently a performer on Contract-Based Development and Adaptive Deployment for Communication Under Contested Environments (DARPA C2E), Formal Knowledge Modeling and Inference for Aircrew Labor In-Cockpit Automation System (DARPA ALIAS) and Modeling and Verification of Human-Machine Systems (ONR).
      • Susmit graduated with a Ph.D. in Dec,2011 from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at University of California, Berkeley and his Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering in 2006 at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur
